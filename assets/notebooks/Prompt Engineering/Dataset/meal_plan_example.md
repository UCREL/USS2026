# Breakfast Meal Plan: Milk Oats with Boiled Eggs and Banana
#### Ingredients
-   Oats 80 g
-   3 Eggs
-   1 Banana
-   Lactose-Free Milk 300 ml
-   Peanut Butter 15 g

#### Preparation Steps
Step1: Cook the oats in the milk until soft. Boil the eggs in the same time. 
Stpe2: Serve the banana whole, and spread the peanut butter on top of the oatmeal.
